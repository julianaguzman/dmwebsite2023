What is Dance Marathon at Cypress Bay High?

Miracle Network Dance Marathon is a program of Children's Miracle Network Hospitals and raises funds for 170 children's hospitals. Donations benefit local member hospitals to fund life-saving treatments, healthcare services, research, pediatric medical equipment, child life services, and financial assistance for families. CBHS DM runs through Nicklaus Children's Hospital which relies on your donations to support the health of future generations.
